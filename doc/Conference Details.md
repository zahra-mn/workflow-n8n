## 6. Halls

### **Overview**

The San Francisco Convention Center features two exhibition halls (**Hall A** and **Hall B**) hosting keynotes, workshops, and exhibitor booths.

### **Hall A**

* **Description:** Primary hall near the main entrance; hosts keynotes and networking.
* **Size:** 10,000 sqft
* **Layout:**

  * Central area: Main Hall (1,500 capacity)
  * Perimeter: Exhibition booths
  * Side rooms: Room A (capacity 200)
* **Location Notes:** High-traffic near main entrance and keynote stage; quieter near Room A.

### **Hall B**

* **Description:** Secondary hall for workshops and specialized exhibits.
* **Size:** 500 sqft
* **Layout:**

  * Central area: Open exhibition space
  * Side rooms: Room B (capacity 150)
* **Location Notes:** Ideal for hands-on demos; moderate traffic in central booths.
